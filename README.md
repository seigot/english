## 文法表現（110点で差がつく構文）

### 論理・評価・因果構文

| 構文 | 用途 | 例文 |
|---|---|---|
| not only A but also B | 論点拡張 | Not only did the policy fail, but it also widened disparities. |
| A comes at the expense of B | トレードオフ | Growth came at the expense of sustainability. |
| To what extent ~ | 程度評価 | To what extent can policy reduce risk? |
| be subject to ~ | 制度対象 | Residents are subject to strict regulation. |
| be attributable to ~ | 原因帰属 | The delay is attributable to bureaucracy. |
